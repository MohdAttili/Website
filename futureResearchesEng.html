Future Studies

Study Description
Lighthouse Installing lights on the generators above the water, which act as a signal, in addition to functioning as a lighthouse that ships can benefit from.
Emergency Point Installing an emergency distress point in case someone gets stuck near the generator in the sea, which sends a signal to the relevant authorities.
Support System Preparing a system to measure the generator's output, peak hours, and real-time production.
Desalination Exploring the potential use of the generated energy in desalinating water: A study on the possibility of using wave-generated energy to operate water desalination plants, especially in areas that suffer from a shortage of fresh water resources. This could provide a sustainable and safe source of water for coastal areas.
