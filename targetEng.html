Target

Recent studies indicate the necessity of eliminating environmentally harmful energy sources and replacing them with modern, environmentally friendly, and sustainable alternatives (United Nations), in line with the 2050 vision for sustainable energy. We also believe in the right of our people in Gaza to live independently, ending the ongoing oppression by the Israeli occupation, and the restrictions imposed on their daily lives, such as reducing the energy available to them and cutting it off entirely at times. The project also aims to establish an innovative system that contributes to the early detection of underwater earthquakes and tsunamis. Generating energy from ocean waves is a relatively new idea, but it faces some environmental challenges.
