We seek to provide a sustainable energy system for the Gaza Strip

Summary:

The project ("Waves for Gaza's Future") aims to design and develop an electricity generator that relies on wave energy as an innovative solution to address the electricity crisis faced by the residents of Gaza due to the Israeli blockade (Palestinian Children's Relief Fund). The project utilizes Gaza's coastal location to convert wave energy into clean and sustainable electricity. The project focuses on enhancing self-sufficiency and supporting the energy infrastructure, contributing to improving the quality of life and meeting the vital needs of the population, while providing an environmentally friendly alternative that reflects a vision of hope and a sustainable future for Gaza, in addition to early tsunami detection and mitigating large waves.
